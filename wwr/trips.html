Check out the trips we have planned

Snake River

This is our most famous and first trip we ever offered. A three day journey down the snake river, with camping along the side each evening. There are many calm and scenic parts to give you a rest after the wild and exciting rapids. It has been our highest rated trip for years, and is great for the more experienced rafter.

Bear River

For those who are just getting started in rafting, this day trip we do multiple times a year is a perfect begginer run. It is a short section of the bear river, not to tiring but still plenty exciting with many quick rapids and beautiful scenery. It's an experience you wont forget!

Rockies River Tour

Finally, for all you adventurous thrill seekers out there, we have a two week long getaway that involves three different rivers in the beautiful rocky mountains. food and camping is provided, and there are rest days, but be prepared to be on the water like never before.
